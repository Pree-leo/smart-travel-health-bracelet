About the Product

The Smart Travel Health & Safety Bracelet is a wearable system designed to enhance safety and health monitoring during travel. It integrates multiple sensors and communication modules that collect physiological and location data from the user.

The system analyzes this information to detect health risks and identify potentially unsafe travel zones, while also providing an emergency SOS response mechanism.

Product Design

The bracelet contains embedded sensors and communication components that allow it to track activity, stress levels, body temperature, location and alert system.

Health Sensors

Tracks heart rate, stress level, sleep patterns and body temperature.

Safety Features

Includes emergency SOS alert system and unsafe zone detection.
