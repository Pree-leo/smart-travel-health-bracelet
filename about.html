About the Product

The Smart Travel Health & Safety Bracelet is a wearable system designed to enhance safety and health monitoring during travel. It integrates multiple sensors and communication modules that collect physiological and location data from the user.

The system analyzes this information to detect health risks and identify potentially unsafe travel zones, while also providing an emergency SOS response mechanism.

Product Design

The bracelet contains embedded sensors and communication components that allow it to track activity, stress levels, body temperature, and location.

Integrated Components

  • Accelerometer Sensor – detects movement and physical activity.
  • Heart Rate Sensor – monitors heart rate and stress indicators.
  • Temperature Sensor – measures body temperature changes.
  • GPS Module – determines the user's current location for safety alerts.
  • Bluetooth Module – transmits sensor data to the monitoring system.
  • Microcontroller – processes sensor data and controls system functions.
  • SOS Emergency Button – allows the user to send an emergency alert instantly.
